About Us

We are pleased to introduce ourselves as specialists in heavy & precision fabrication work since 2021, We have carried out several jobs in Glasswool, manufacturing and Chemical industries in MS, SS. We have executed heavy turnkey jobs including erection at site. Shanti Industrial Services have sound financial background and undertake fabrication work with own raw material of MS & SS in different sizes. We have also introduced Special purpose jobs to our existing range of precised fabrication and machining. Our Factory shed is totally covered & has a clear lifting height of 10 meters with a truss height of 13 meters. We have vast experience in heavy & precision fabrication as well as in erection work.
